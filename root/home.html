what is home to you? where is home to you? Is it where you wake up every morning, or where you think about before you go to bed every night?

As the child of a Syrian father and Palestinian mother, and grandchild to refugees. I often think about where home is to me.

Home to me; it is somewhere i have never been. It is somewhere i see only from behind a screen. On the news, my social media feed, or any other outlet that lets me know how its burning.

I would like to share with you the experiences of Palestinians who still call it home, past and present.
